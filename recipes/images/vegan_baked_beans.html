Vegan Baked Beans

Homemade baked beans, done quickly in the pressure cooker. Great on toast with sauted mushrooms and a small side salad

Ingredients:

  • olive oil
  • medium Onion, minced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 t paprika
  • 1/4 C veggie broth
  • 1 lb small white beans (navy or great northern) about 2 Cups
  • 4 C veggie broth
  • 3 T brown mustard
  • 1 C ketchup
  • 1/2 C Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 C molasses
  • 3 T apple cider vinegar
  • 1 serrano1

1 Optional - We like it spicy, so I use the whole serrano with seeds; adjust to your spice level. You can use red Chilli flakes if you prefer.

Instructions

  1. Saute onion, garlic, and serrano, in pressure cooker, for about 5 minutes. until onions start to turn translucent.
  2. add paprika and 1/4 cup broth. continue to saute for 1 to 2 minutes
  3. Add remaining ingedients
  4. Pressure cook on high or steady rock for 75 minutes
  5. Natural release and enjoy!
