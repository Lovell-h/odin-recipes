Stir Fry Sauce

Homemade Stir Fry Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 C veggie broth
  • 1/3 C soy sauce or amino acids
  • 2 T honey
  • 2 t sesame oil
  • 2 T Fresh grated ginger
  • 1 T Cornstarch or arrow root
  • red pepper flakes (to taste)
  • sweet chili oil (to taste)

    • Instructions

    1. combine all ingredients in a mason or other jar with a sealing lid
    2. shake until combined
    3. refrigerate until ready to use
    4. keeps well in the refrigerator for 1 to 2 weeks
